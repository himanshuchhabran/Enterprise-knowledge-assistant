# Nebula9.ai Leave Policy

**Annual Leave:**
All full-time employees are entitled to 25 paid vacation days per year. Part-time employees receive a pro-rata entitlement. Unused days cannot be carried over to the next year without explicit manager approval.

**Sick Leave:**
Employees are entitled to 10 paid sick days per year. A doctor's note is required for absences longer than two consecutive days.

**Remote Work Policy:**
Employees can work remotely up to 3 days per week. A formal request must be submitted to your line manager. Core working hours are 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM.
